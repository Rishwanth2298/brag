What is the concept of digital marketing?

Digital marketing, commonly known as web marketing, is the advertising of products across the website and other means of digital contact in order to communicate with future consumers. As a marketing channel, which covers not just Facebook, social networking, and web-based advertisements, but also text and interactive communications. Internet marketing vs. inbound marketing

For good cause, digital marketing and inbound marketing are often confused. Many of the same techniques that are used in inbound marketing are used in digital marketing, including email and web material, to name a couple. Both operate to catch prospects' interest and convert them into buyers during the buyer's path. However, the two perspectives have opposing viewpoints on the tool-goal partnership. Each tool in digital marketing is evaluated for its ability to convert prospects. A company's digital marketing strategies can include several channels or rely solely on one. Inbound marketing is a broad term that encompasses a variety of strategies. It starts with the mission, then examines the available resources to see the ones that can successfully attract target buyers, and finally, at which point in the sales funnel this should occur. In reality, they're most effective when they're working together. Inbound marketing gives direction and intent to digital marketing activities, ensuring that each digital marketing platform is working against a common target.

Assets for Digital Marketing

A digital marketing asset can be almost everything. It actually needs to be an online marketing method. However, several consumers are unaware of the wealth of digital marketing resources available to them. Here are a few examples:

  1. Your online presence
  2. Identifiable properties (logos, icons, acronyms, etc)
  3. Video-based material (video ads, product demos, etc)
  4. Photographs (infographics, product shots, company photos, etc)
  5. Textual material (blog posts, eBooks, product descriptions, testimonials, etc)
  6. Online software or goods (SaaS, calculators, interactive content, etc)
  7. Social media accounts
  8. Reviews

Like you would expect, this list just scratches the surface. The majority of digital marketing assets will fall into one of these groups, but as creative marketers continue to come up with innovative ways to target consumers digitally, the list will continue to expand!

Strategies for Digital Marketing

While the list of digital marketing tactics is continually changing, the following are some of the more popular strategies used by most businesses:

No-Per-Click (PPC) Marketing
PPC advertisement is a general concept that refers to any form of digital marketing in which you pay for each consumer that clicks on an ad. Google AdWords, for example, is a form of PPC advertisement known as "paying search advertising" (which we'll discuss in a moment). Facebook Ads are a form of PPC advertisement known as "paying social network advertising".
Search Engine Marketing (SEM)
You will run text advertisements on Google, Bing, and Yahoo's Search Engine Results Pages (SERPs). Paid search ads is one of the most effective ways to reach out to those who are specifically looking for a good or service similar to yours.
SEO is for "search engine optimisation" (SEO)
You may use search engine optimization (SEO) to attempt to rate pages or blog entries on your web organically if you don't want to pay to be in the SERPs. You don't have to pay for any click, but having a page to rank normally takes a long time and a lot of work (for a more in-depth comparison of paid search and SEO, check out this article).
Paid Advertising on Social Media
You can run advertisements on most social networking sites, including Facebook, Instagram, Twitter, LinkedIn, Pinterest, and Snapchat. Paid social networking advertisement is excellent for raising visibility among viewers that are unfamiliar with your business, commodity, or service.
Marketing on Social Media
Social media marketing, like SEO, is a free, natural way to advertise your company on social media sites like Facebook and Twitter. Organically selling the company on social media, like SEO, requires a lot of time and money upfront, so it will pay off in the long run.
Optimization of Conversion Rates (CRO)
The art and science of optimizing your web customer experience is known as conversion rate optimization (CRO). CRO is more often used by companies to increase conversions (leads, chats, calls, orders, and so on) from their current website traffic.

Marketing of Content

Another broad digital marketing concept is content marketing. Any digital marketing initiative that utilizes content assets (blog posts, infographics, eBooks, images, and so on) to create brand recognition or generate clicks, leads, or purchases is referred to as content marketing.

Advertisement that is native to the area

Have you ever been to the bottom of an essay and see a page of recommended reading? That's what native ads are all about. Since it uses the material to draw clicks ("you'll never believe what comes next!"), most native ads fall under the category of content marketing. Since the native advertisement is generally blended in with non-paid content recommendations, it may be difficult to spot...but that's sort of the idea.

Email marketing is the oldest and most successful method of internet marketing. Email marketing is commonly used by digital marketers to sell promotional offers, showcase news (often as part of content marketing), or promote a cause.
